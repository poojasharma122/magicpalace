Magic Palace – Gaming & Entertainment in Montréal

Step into the excitement at Magic Palace, a gaming and entertainment destination just minutes from downtown Montréal. Known for our cutting-edge gaming machines, great food, and buzzing atmosphere, we offer a complete experience for casual visitors and regular players alike.

Whether you're planning a night out, trying your luck at our machines, or looking for somewhere to unwind, Magic Palace has it all under one roof.

Play Over 400 Electronic Games

Explore one of the largest collections of electronic gaming machines on the South Shore. With more than 400 options ranging from classic slots to today’s most advanced multi-line and themed games, you’ll find something that fits your style of play — and your chance to win big.

Machines are updated frequently to include new titles and popular fan favourites. Our floor is laid out for comfort and accessibility, with attentive staff always nearby.

Dine In or Drop By for a Bite

Our full-service restaurant offers a relaxed setting for meals, drinks, and casual meetups. With a carefully selected menu of fresh, locally sourced dishes, it’s a great option for dinner before your games or a late-night snack.

Group bookings and private dining options are available — just ask a member of our team for details.

Weekly Events & Live Entertainment

Keep an eye on our events calendar for live music nights, guest DJs, seasonal parties, and prize draws. Magic Palace is more than just a gaming spot — it’s a place to socialise, unwind, and enjoy the buzz of live energy.

Looking for something special? Many of our themed events include exclusive games, food specials, and Loyalty Program perks.

Loyalty Program Perks

Our Loyalty Program rewards you every time you play. Earn points, unlock exclusive promotions, and enjoy members-only benefits across the venue. Sign up in person or ask at the front desk during your next visit.
